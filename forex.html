What is Forex?

Forex, or the foreign exchange market, involves the trading of currencies. Unlike the stock market, forex trading operates 24/7, allowing you to trade whenever it suits you. The goal is to profit from the fluctuations in currency values.

Why Trade Forex?

  • High Liquidity: The forex market is the most liquid market in the world, making it easy to enter and exit trades quickly.
  • 24/7 Market: Forex operates around the clock, allowing you to trade at your convenience.
  • Leverage: Forex brokers offer leverage, allowing you to control larger positions with a smaller amount of capital.
